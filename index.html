01 · About

Engineering, measured.

I am a PhD student in industrial engineering at Politehnica University of Timișoara, working at the intersection of injection molding of technical polymers, additive manufacturing and dimensional metrology — coordinate measuring machines (CMM), 3D scanning and design of experiments (DoE).

My research asks a simple question with an expensive answer: why should the dimensional accuracy of an injection-molded part depend on years of operator experience? I am developing a measurement-driven, mathematical route to dimensional optimization — a workflow meant to take a newcomer from first shot to in-tolerance parts, guided by data and models instead of trial and error.

The method itself will be disclosed the proper way — through peer-reviewed publications (in preparation). This site documents the journey: articles, conference posters, teaching materials and, soon, open-source Python tools developed along the way.

02 · Research areas

What I work on

R-01

Injection molding of technical polymers

Process parameters, semi-crystalline polymers, mold design and the dimensional quality of injected parts.

R-02

Additive manufacturing (MEX/FDM)

Material extrusion of engineering thermoplastics and metrology-driven dimensional optimization of printed parts.

R-03

Dimensional metrology — CMM & 3D scanning

Coordinate measuring machines, GD&T evaluation, measurement strategy and statistical process capability.

R-04

Industry 4.0 & digital twins

Data-driven process monitoring, design of experiments (DoE) and virtual replicas of manufacturing processes.
