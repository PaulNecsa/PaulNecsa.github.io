Free tools for an engineering PhD

A curated toolbox I use and recommend — injection molding, additive manufacturing, 3D metrology, design of experiments (DoE), literature work and academic writing. Every "free" claim below was checked against official sources; free tiers change, so treat the date above as the expiry check.

free = fully free / open source freemium = usable free tier edu = free with university email
starter kit literature writing DoE & stats CAD & FEM molding sim metrology & AM materials publishing figures & org Industry 4.0 hidden gems

00 · Starter kit

If you install only ten things

01 · Zotero 7 + Better BibTeXfree

Reference manager for the whole thesis; stable citation keys and auto-updating .bib export for LaTeX.

02 · ZEISS INSPECT freefree tier

Ex-GOM Inspect: scan-vs-CAD deviation maps, mesh editing, sections, reports — the de facto standard in metrology papers.

03 · Moldflow Adviser (Education)edu

The realistic free path to mold-filling simulation: 1-year renewable education license, material database included.

04 · R: FrF2 + rsm + qccfree

The free Minitab replacement: fractional factorial DoE, response surfaces, control charts, Cp/Cpk.

05 · PrePoMax + CalculiXfree

Serious FEM on Windows with zero node limits — static, nonlinear and thermal studies, STEP import.

06 · FreeCAD 1.1free

Parametric CAD with no restrictions, ever — parts, fixtures, drawings, plus an integrated FEM workbench.

07 · CloudComparefree

Point-cloud and mesh comparison (C2C/C2M), ICP registration, Python batch processing of scans.

08 · ResearchRabbit + Semantic Scholarfree

Literature discovery that stays free: collections, alerts, citation graphs, Zotero sync.

09 · Overleaf + local TeX Livefreemium

Papers on Overleaf free tier; the thesis compiles locally (free tier now limits compiles to 10 s, 1 collaborator).

10 · Zenodo + Open Policy Finderfree

Free DOIs for datasets, STL files and posters + journal self-archiving policies before you upload any PDF.

01 · Literature & references

Find, map and manage the literature

Semantic Scholarfree

Semantic search, TLDR summaries, citation graphs; free API for scripted searches.

OpenAlexfree

Open bibliometric database (CC0). API needs a free key since Feb 2026 — generous daily allowance.

ResearchRabbitfree

"Free forever" discovery: seed collections, weekly alerts, author networks, Zotero sync.

Connected Papersfreemium

Visual graphs of prior and derivative works; 5 free graphs per month.

Elicitfreemium

AI-assisted screening and data extraction from papers; free Basic plan (no RIS/BIB export).

Google Scholar + alertsfree

The safety net: unlimited keyword and author alerts catch what curated databases miss.

Scopus Previewfreemium

Free without subscription: author profiles, h-index, CiteScore — verify target journal indexing and quartiles.

CORE + BASEfree

Open-access full text and European grey literature (theses, reports) — strong for metrology topics.

Unpaywallfree

Browser extension: one green button to the legal open-access version of paywalled papers.

Zotero 7 + Better BibTeXfree

Open-source reference manager; 300 MB file sync free (unlimited locally or via WebDAV).

JabReffree

Native BibTeX editor — a clean backup workflow for the thesis .bib files.

Scitepaid now

Supporting/contrasting citation analysis. No free plan anymore (7-day trial only) — listed so you don't plan around it.

02 · Writing

Write and polish manuscripts

Overleaffreemium

Collaborative LaTeX. Free tier (2026): unlimited projects but 10 s compile timeout and 1 collaborator — fine for papers, not the thesis.

TeX Live + VS Codefree

Local LaTeX with zero limits; same source as Overleaf. The thesis-safe plan B.

Typstfreemium

Modern typesetting with instant compile; open-source compiler runs locally free, growing IEEE/Elsevier template pool.

LanguageToolfreemium

Grammar/style checker (EN + RO): free web editor up to 10,000 characters per check; browser extension is premium-only now.

Writefullfreemium

Language feedback trained on scientific text, in Word and Overleaf; daily free quota — ask your library about an institutional license.

03 · DoE & statistics

Design of experiments and process statistics

R: FrF2 + DoE.base + rsm + qccfree

Full and fractional factorials, Taguchi, response surface methodology, control charts and Cp/Cpk — the publishable Minitab replacement.

Python: pyDOE3 + statsmodelsfree

Experimental designs (factorial, CCD, Box-Behnken, LHS) plus ANOVA/regression inside your data pipeline.

JASPfree

Point-and-click statistics with publication-ready output; Quality Control module covers 2^k designs and capability.

jamovifree

SPSS-like GUI over R; community DoE module: factorial, Box-Behnken, CCD, mixture, Taguchi.

G*Powerfree

Sample-size and power analysis before committing machine time to an experimental campaign.

04 · CAD & FEM

Model and simulate

FreeCAD 1.1free

Parametric CAD, unrestricted STEP/STL export, integrated FEM workbench; v1.1 improved assemblies and meshing.

Onshape EDUedu

Cloud CAD; unlike the public free plan, education documents stay private — better for unpublished research.

PrePoMax + CalculiXfree

The fastest route to serious free FEM on Windows: portable GUI, STEP import, Netgen/Gmsh meshing.

Ansys Student 2026edu

Mechanical, Fluent, Polyflow, optiSLang — free and renewable; limits: 128 K nodes structural, 1 M cells fluids.

Abaqus Learning Editionedu

Free but capped at 1,000 nodes — for learning the workflow, not for thesis-scale models.

Code_Aster / Elmer FEMfree

Advanced open-source multiphysics (nonlinear, thermo-mechanical); steeper learning curve, Linux-native.

05 · Injection molding simulation

Mold-filling simulation — the honest picture

Honest note (July 2026): there is no mold-filling simulator that is simultaneously free-forever, actively maintained and industrial-grade. The realistic free route for research is an education license; the open-source route exists but is aging. Both are listed below.
Moldflow Adviser Ultimate (EDU)edu

Filling, packing, weld lines, air traps + rheological material database (Cross-WLF, pvT). Free 1-year education license, renewable while enrolled.

openInjMoldSim (OpenFOAM)free

Open-source filling/packing solver (GPL-3), citable and modifiable — but unmaintained since Nov 2021; for principle studies.

Ansys Polyflow (in Student)edu

Viscoelastic polymer flows (extrusion, blow molding) — not dedicated mold filling, but free and legitimate.

Moldex3Dtrial only

No public student edition; 30-day trial plus free e-learning ("Moldex3D University") for training.

06 · 3D metrology & additive manufacturing

Measure, inspect, print

ZEISS INSPECT freefree tier

Scan-vs-CAD colour deviation maps, alignments, mesh editing, sections and reports; advanced GD&T filters only in the pro trial.

CloudComparefree

C2C/C2M distances, ICP registration, scalar fields; CloudComPy scripts batch-process whole scan campaigns.

MeshLabfree

Mesh cleanup, repair and decimation before inspection or printing.

PrusaSlicerfree

Reference slicer for MEX/FDM studies — clean parameter control for DoE on printing parameters.

OrcaSlicerfree

Built-in systematic calibrations (flow, pressure advance, temperature towers) — ideal for printer characterization.

ExifToolfree

Read/clean metadata of images and PDFs before manuscript submission.

07 · Polymer material data

Datasheets you can actually compare

CAMPUS Plasticsfree

~4,600 grades from 27 producers, strictly ISO 10350/11403 data — comparable single- and multi-point curves (PA, PP, PBT, PEEK…).

UL Prospectorfreemium

The widest commercial-grade coverage (43k+ datasheets); free account with viewing limits.

MatWebfreemium

Quick generic property lookup across material families; free with registration.

08 · Publishing, data & integrity

Publish right, share data, stay clean

Open Policy Finderfree

Ex-Sherpa Romeo: check each journal's self-archiving policy before uploading any PDF to a personal site.

DOAJ + Think.Check.Submitfree

Verify open-access journals are legitimate; checklist against predatory venues.

ORCID + Crossreffree

Researcher ID (displayed as full URL) + DOI metadata verification for reference lists.

Zenodofree

CERN-hosted repository: free DOIs and versioning for datasets, STL/G-code, posters — up to 50 GB per record.

OSFfree

Organize the whole doctoral project, preregister studies; 50 GB per public project.

engrXivfree

Engineering-dedicated preprint server with active moderation.

Wayback Machine + archive.todayfree

Archive every web page you cite (datasheets, product pages) — the anti-link-rot insurance for the thesis.

Forensically + TinEyefree

Clone detection / error level analysis and reverse image search — audit your composed figures before submission.

09 · Figures, posters & organization

Make it look right, keep it on track

Inkscape + GIMPfree

Vector figures (process schematics, experimental setups) and micrograph processing — journal-DPI exports, no watermarks.

draw.io / diagrams.netfree

Flowcharts and methodology diagrams, no account needed, exports SVG/PDF.

Veusz / matplotlib / TikZfree

Publication plots: GUI (Veusz), scripted (matplotlib), or typographically native in LaTeX (TikZ).

LibreOffice Impress/Drawfree

A0 conference posters and presentations, print-quality PDF export. (Note: Canva's education plan excludes universities.)

Better Poster templatesfree

Poster formats engineered for legibility at conference distance.

Notion Education Plusedu

Free Plus plan with a university email: paper pipeline, experiment log, deadlines.

Obsidianfree

Local Markdown knowledge base (free even commercially since 2025); links to Zotero via community plugins.

10 · Industry 4.0 & digital twins

Instrument the process

Node-REDfree

Low-code data flows from machines and sensors (OPC UA, MQTT) — wiring a lab cell without custom code.

Grafana OSS + InfluxDB OSSfree

Time-series storage and live dashboards for process data (temperatures, injection pressures).

Eclipse Dittofree

Actively maintained open-source digital twin framework (v3.9, 2026).

open62541 / Prosys OPC UA Simulatorfree

Open-source OPC UA stack + freeware simulation server for Industry 4.0 demonstrators.

11 · Hidden gems

Unexpected finds that earn their place

Discovered while auditing a large OSINT tool list — most of it is irrelevant to research, but these few are quietly excellent for academic work.

Espacenetfree

~150 million patents (EPO): state of the art for mold tooling, conformal cooling and metrology fixtures — patents often precede papers.

Filmotfree

Searches inside YouTube subtitles: find the exact minute a term is discussed in conference talks and vendor webinars.

waybackpyfree

Python: archive every URL in your bibliography to the Wayback Machine and get permanent links back.

Talkwalker Alertsfree

Field monitoring alongside Google Alerts — catches industry blogs and niche press the other misses.

Google Dataset Searchfree

Find published datasets (process parameters, point clouds) to benchmark against your own.

DeepLfreemium

Strong technical translation — unlocks the German injection molding literature (Kunststoffe).
